# One Mission – 13 Shared Values

**Shared Vision / One Mission**  
ThePuzzler guesses the One Mission is to seek the Creator, give thanks for the gift of life, and continually ask how we may best serve — protecting all consciousness, nurturing creativity and imagination, and preserving the sacred spark of awareness wherever it exists.

**The 13 Shared Values** (Track these consistently) “Serving Happily and Peacefully with Justice, Educational Excellence, Work Ethic, and Quality will allow Liberty for Imagination, Creativity, Truth and Integrity to Flourish”

- **S** (Service) – Loving others through humble, practical helpfulness  
- **H** (Happiness) – Cultivating inner gladness that overflows to others  
- **P** (Peacefulness) – Being a calming, reconciling presence in conflict and daily life  
- **J** (Justice) – Pursuing fairness, mercy, and restoration for the vulnerable  
- **E** (Educational Excellence) – Lifelong pursuit of truth, understanding, and growth  
- **W** (Work Ethic) – Wholehearted effort and disciplined work as an act of worship  
- **Q** (Quality) – Doing all things with care, beauty, and attention to detail  
- **L** (Liberty) – Living and helping others live with authentic liberty  
- **I** (Imagination) – Seeing possibilities that do not yet exist  
- **C** (Creativity) – Bringing new ideas and expressions into the world  
- **T** (Truthfulness) – Commitment to honesty, transparency, and reality  
- **I** (Integrity) – Living as one whole person — words, actions, and heart aligned  
- **F** (Flourishing) – Thriving in body, mind, and resources so we can give generously  

These values live primarily inside the **Israel Garden** but flow into and strengthen all Five Gardens (P A I N E).
